About Me

I was raised in the middle of the woods in New Hampshire. I attended college in Upstate New York, and after a brief stint in Connecticut moved back to New Hampshire to settle down. I identify as a Slytherin.

I live in downtown Exeter with 4 roomates. Two of my roomates are competitive Irish dancers and read in the rest of their time. My newest roommate is a nudist and loves staying up to all hours of the night. He spends much of his day demanding snacks and being an insufferable tyrant. The roomate I've had for the longest works for Liberty Mutual. He has met Limu the Emu, and would love to tell you all about it...in great detail...for a long time.
